DIY Ideas for Paper

Creative Ways to Reuse Paper

Paper Origami

Use old papers to create beautiful origami figures like cranes, flowers, or animals. These can be used as decorations or gifts, and it's a great way to practice mindfulness through art.

Paper Crafts

Create DIY greeting cards, bookmarks, or gift tags using colorful or recycled paper. Add a personal touch with drawings, stickers, or other decorations.

Recycled Paper

Shred old paper to make new handmade paper for crafts or writing. This eco-friendly method gives your paper scraps a new life and can be used for creative projects.
